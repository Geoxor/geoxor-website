Biography

George Tsotsos (born 27 March 2001), better known as Geoxor (commonly misspelled as Geoxer), is a Greek music producer from Florina, Greece.

As an independent producer Geoxor is influenced by Panda Eyes, Virtual Riot, Porter Robinson, Skrillex, and many others, along with having an anime theme throughout his artwork

In 2016 NoCopyrightSounds signed his single “You & I” and has surpassed 10 million views on YouTube.

In 2017 he released multiple singles along with his first EP called “Funky World” featuring 3 tunes one of which DSG promoted on YouTube.

In 2018 he released his second EP called “Time of Waste” as a pun directed towards his real life events that were happening as he was making the EP.

In 2019 he started off the year by releasing “Stardust” a catchy video game style tune, which DSG had promoted on his YouTube, getting up to 1+ million views within 3 months, further more having Knife Party’s support @ ULTRA 2019 with the tune “Nana”
